~ About Us ~

Banasthali University has been at the forefront of women's education since its establishment in 1935. Spanning an expansive 850 acres, the campus boasts a multitude of buildings, each varying in stature and size, encompassing diverse academic and administrative blocks. The campus is strategically organized into distinct divisions, namely the University division, School division, and residential blocks. This thoughtful layout ensures the fulfillment of essential needs for its residents. The institution's commitment to providing a conducive environment for education and living is reflected in its well-planned and comprehensive campus infrastructure.
Let us take you towards the campus amenities in detail
Accommodation
Banasthali University stands as a residential haven, boasting almost 50 hostels designed to accommodate between 110 to 438 students each. The institution ensures that the power of the Internet is readily available to all residents, with an extensive network providing unlimited online opportunities. Furthermore, the university offers over 400 staff quarters, ranging from one-storeyed houses to modern multi-storeyed apartments. This diverse array of accommodations caters to the needs of the faculty, providing a comfortable living environment. Additionally, a dedicated Working Women’s Hostel with 113 seats has been established to support the accommodation needs of female staff members. In essence, Banasthali University prioritizes not only academic excellence but also ensures a well-equipped and connected living space for both students and staff, fostering a conducive environment for holistic development.
Sports
Banasthali University boasts excellent sports infrastructure, including the Laxmi Bai and Vidula Maidans for diverse activities. The Laxmi Bai Maidan, spanning 26,690 sq. meters, accommodates parades, fairs, cultural events, and film screenings. Vidula Maidan, covering 41,950 sq. meters, hosts various sports like hockey, volleyball, basketball, tennis, yoga, martial arts, and indoor games. The campus also features a swimming pool open to students and staff. Notably, horse riding is a long-standing tradition with 41 horses at the 'Veer Bala Maidan' (25,390 sq. meters). Continuous upgrades ensure high-quality facilities, including the "Shakuntlam Play Ground," a Cricket Ground inaugurated in 2008 by the University President.
Air Strip
There is a licensed air strip of 50,191,418 sq. meters in the Banasthali University which is used for training the students in gliding and flying. This has been one of the activities introduced as early as 1960 even before Banasthali got the University status. A regular flying club, BVGFC (Banasthali Vidyapith Gliding and Flying Club) has started on January 9, 2009.
Central Library
The Central Library at Banasthali University provides Internet access at around 8 Mbps through BSNL and VSNL leased lines. It utilizes the LIBSYS Integrated Library Management software, encompassing all library housekeeping modules. The online Catalogue (OPAC) holds over 1.60 lakhs bibliographic records and 13,500 back volumes of periodicals. With 600 periodical titles, 550 are subscribed, and the rest are received gratis. Additionally, around 9,000 titles are accessible online through the UGC-INFONET Consortia.
FM Radio Station
A ‘Community FM Radio Station’ known as ‘Radio Banasthali’ broadcasts educational and entertainment programmes locally recorded by the students and staff of Banasthali. It operates at 90.4 MHz and serves the campus and the community within a radius of 30 km around Banasthali.
Guest House
Round the year there are guests in campus on official assignments .The parents and guardians of the students and visitors are provided accommodation in the well- maintained and fully- furnished university guest house. The staff here works on the principle of ATITHI DEVO BHAVAH.
Transport
Minibuses are run as shuttle service at the interval of 15 minutes. Bus is also provided to the Banasthali-Newai Railway station 7 kms. away from the campus. The Rajasthan Roadways provides regular to and fro bus service between Jaipur and the campus via Newai almost every hour from 6.00 am to 6.00 pm.
IT infrastructure
The IT infrastructure at Banasthali is comparable to any world class university. Apart from the state of the art Servers and Workstations at Apaji institute of Mathematics & Applied Computer Technology, every department and office of Banasthali has adequate computing facilities along with high bandwidth Internet access which is provided through a fibre optic Campus Network. Artech-Dalmia I.T. Center has increased this IT infrastructure manyfold. The center has been constructed with partial financial assistance from Shri M.H. Dalmia and Artech LTD. The Centre has smart class rooms and high end computing laboratories for Computer Aided Design, Scientific Computing, Microelectronics & VLSI design, and Computational Biology.
Besides these facilities, the following amenities are available at the campus: a. UCO Bank, Co-operative Bank, State Bank of India, UCO Bank ATM, SBI ATM, ICICI Bank ATM b. Post office with a facility of Speed Post c. BSNL Electronic Exchange d. Courier Service e. Fully equipped shopping complexes and vegetable marts
